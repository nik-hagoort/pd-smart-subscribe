Smart Subscribe
How to Use:
Navigate to the PagerDuty status page
Check the box next to any Business Service(s) that you would like to subscribe to
Click the green "Subscribe to Selected Services" button in the bottom right corner of your page
You'll be redirected to your subscription page where services will be automatically added
Tip: Want to subscribe to all of the Business Services? Click the blue "Select All" button without checking any boxes
Need assistance?
Contact the SRE team via the "Get Help" channel in Zoom!
